Learnings & reflection
  • How to effectively translate users value, and business priorities from larger product ecosystem to build a cross-product vision, alignment, and roadmap across multiple organizations.

  • One successful strategy to influence and effectively proposing solutions to stakeholders or leadership teams is to understand their goals and priorities. I learned that oftentimes we all have the same customer goals. But, the route and methods we take could be differently. Emphasize on common goals and collaboratively walk towards that goals together.

  • North start vision is really important to help show a product's potential value, drive alignments, and get stakeholder buy-in. Partner teams use the north star to understand a product's vision, value, and how it'll impact customers. Driving clarity and alignment through collaboration

  • Making people willing to say yes and be able to say yes on the product execution are completely different stories. Sometimes, it really depends on the right timing and conditions. Don't be afraid of "No" and do ask how can we make this this happens together as a team.
