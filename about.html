About me, June Punkasem

I'm a visionary and interdisciplinary product designer who believe in data-informed design approaches and collaborative team culture. Using a human-centered approach, I love to design and build digital products that improve people's daily interactions with technology.

I'm currently working as a Senior design lead at Microsoft Data cloud studio, and also help designing online multiplayer retro gaming platforms at Piepacker. Before joining Microsoft, I pursued Master of Human Centered Design and Engineering at the University of Washington and also worked as a UX Engineer and UX Designer at several start ups such as INRIX, and Pecan Analytics.

Previously, I have trained as an urban geographer and spatial analyst during my undergraduate at Chulalongkorn University in Bangkok. I then further pursued my passion for human-spatial interaction at Louisiana State University and graduated in 2016 with a Master of Science in Geography.
